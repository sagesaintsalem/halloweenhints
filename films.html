Would you like to play a game? Interactivity makes things scarier! Starting with the classics and modern classics, why not check out the Resident Evil series? From the original 1997 game with Leon and Claire to the recent Resident Evil Village with a giant vampire lady that had the internet drooling, there's something gross and scary jumping out at you with every installment!

Another popular series is Silent Hill. Find your daughter, Cheryl, or watch a man with a pyramid for a head commit unspeakable acts on mannequins! Earlier games are atmospheric and rely on the player's imagination to fill in the blanks with the scariest outcome, while later games are more jump-scare heavy. Although, Shattered Memories tries to psychoanalyse the player and whisper through the controller...there's a different flavour of scary in every game!

Lovecraftian monsters and a ridiculous high difficulty? What could be scarier?! Bloodborne is a game from the popular FromSoftware studio, known for their frustratingly difficult games and subtle storytelling, where you fight your way through all manner of incomprehensible horrors with improbable weapons (my favourite is the cane-whip). Happy hunting!
